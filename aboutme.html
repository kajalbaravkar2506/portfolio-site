ABOUT ME

CREATIVE PORTFOLIO
Hello! I am Kajal Baravkar, a 2 nd year Computer Science Engineering student at Vellore Institute of Technology. I have a deep interest in both web development and graphic design, and I enjoy combining technology with creativity to craft clean, bold, and visually impactful experiences. My work allows me to bridge logic and aesthetics — whether I'm coding an interface or designing a brand identity.

Currently, I am working with Vertex as a Creative Graphic Designer, where I get to experiment with fresh ideas and challenge the boundaries of conventional design. It's a space where I grow, collaborate, and bring concepts to life through visual storytelling.

This portfolio is a reflection of my journey so far — a place to showcase the skills I am building, and the passion that drives me. And this is just the beginning!
